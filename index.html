End of the year contest

The first edition of the End of the year AI programming contest will be held in a couple of weeks! The challenge is to code a bot for a game in a limited amount of time. You can work in teams of 2-3 persons on one (provided) laptop, without internet access. The game you will code a bot for will be revealed on the day itself. Afterwards there's a free (!) barbecue and during the barbecue a tournament is shown on screens where your bot will compete against the other teams. Interested? Read on for the practical details!

Practical

The contest will take place on June 21, during the exam weeks and can be a nice distraction from exam learning.

The contest is free of charge and there will be a free barbecue provided at the end with 1 free drink.

Contest location is the Flux building, barbecue is held in front of MetaForum.

Questions can be send to contest2016@hsaconfluente.nl . Or you can register now!
